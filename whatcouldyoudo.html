Multi-faith Peace Society at CRGS

What could you do?

Our main goal at CRGS is to promote peace throughout the school and across the Colchester Community with differnet faiths and beliefs. We also work together with the Community such as Colchester Council to organise and promote peace. In order to achieve these goals, there are many suggestions and prompts on what you could possibly do:



  • Respect religious differences,act on deeply held and widely share values and honour the identity and community of each religious tradition

  • Attend and contribute to our society meetings

  • Make a personal commitment to nonviolence behaviours

  • Organize a community projects to protect our environment (clean up, plant a garden)

  • Recognise that social inequalities are unjust and fuel misery, conflict and violence

  • Develop your understanding of the strong emotions that underpin conflict and violence

  • Share on social media promoting peace or join socities

  • Attend peaceful and non-violent rallies
