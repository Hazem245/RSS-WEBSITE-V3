Multi-faith peace society at CRGS

What could you do?

At CRGS Multi-Faith Peace Society, our main goal is to promote peace throughout the school and across the Colchester Community with differnet faiths and beliefs and work together like a true society. There are many differnet ways you as an individual can promote peace and as a society. Here are a few suggestions and prompts on what you could possibly do:

  • Post on social media promoting peace or join socities

  • Attend peace rallies

  • Attend our socities and meetings and bring family along

  • Make a personal commitment to nonviolence

  • Organize a community project (clean up, plant a garden)

  • Recognise that social inequalities are unjust and fuel misery, conflict and violence

  • Maintain a critical perspective toward the beliefs and actions of your own group

  • Develop your understanding of the strong emotions that underpin conflict and violence
