Multi-faith peace society at CRGS

Our Purpose, Mission and Values

Purpose

Multi-faith peace society at CRGS provides opportunities for all people regarless of faith and religion to talk to other people of differnet ethnicities and cultres to engage together and discuss peace at CRGS and across the globe and how we can provide peace for areas with conflict, contributing to a richer and happier society that we live in today.

Mission

We strive to encourage students and staff from differnet backgrounds to fufil their potential and encourage them to discuss about what is most important and make a change in the world. The Mutli-faith Peace society at Colchester Royal Grammar School hopes to achieve opportunities for communication and relationship building between people of different faiths and enable people of diverse communities and traditions to engage together and discuss peace and work together to make CRGS and the globe free of violence.

Values

Our values support our purpose and we look forward, anticipate what's changing, and embrace the new and the past with energy and imagination and wish to share it with others. One of our main values and principles is to respect religious differences,act on deeply held and widely share values and honour the identity and community of each religious tradition. These principles are what our religious leaders share.
