Multi-faith Peace Society at CRGS

Vision, Mission, Values and Purpose

Vision

Peaceful school and local community in Colchester.

Mission

We hope to achieve opportunities for communication and build peacful relationships among people of all faiths and cultures through education, open discussion,and action.

Values

Our values support our mission and we look forward, anticipate what's changing, and embrace the new and the past with energy and imagination and wish to share it with others. One of our main values and principles is to respect religious differences, act on deeply held and widely share values and honour the identity and community of each religious tradition.

Purpose

Our purpose aims to provide a platform for peace-related activities for the community and the school. In addition, we aim to liaise with others working for peace.
